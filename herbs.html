Company Profile

Herbs Garden Company Is One Of The Most Important Companies Exporting Medicinal And Aromatic Plants And Herbs. It Also Exports Organic, Vital And Ordinary Herbs And Spices As Well, Such As Hibiscus - Basil - Mint - Cumin - Marjoram - Dried Black And Yellow Lemon - Molokhia Paper And Crushed - Dill. Dried - Dried Parsley - Dried Coriander And All Kinds Of Spices And Herbs With International Specifications


Herbs Garden Is One Of The Largest Production Companies In The Arab Republic Of Egypt And Is Also Ready To Implement All Customer Requirements With The Required Specifications And Deal With All Companies And Institutions In This Field As An Intermediary Also In Providing All Products To Global Markets
